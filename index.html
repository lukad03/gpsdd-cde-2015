Global Partnership for Sustainable Development Data (GPSDD)

Community Data Event

  • Sept 18th - 19th

Join scientists, issue area experts, entrepreneurs, and last-mile users to discuss, plan, and build solutions that use data and public information to solve problems outlined in the Global Goals for Sustainable Development. Experts are gathering in person and virtually to share significant gaps in data that affect some of the most intractable problems of climate, gender, agriculture, and health. The diverse gathering will work together on strategies and partnerships to solve defined problems and unleash data to be used toward long term sustainable solutions.
